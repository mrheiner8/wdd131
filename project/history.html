Ascend Choir & Orchestra

Our Mission

Our mission at Ascend Choir and Orchestra is guided by our ABCs:

  • Accessible: We provide opportunities that take into account family schedules and budgets.
  • Balance: We care about more than just time and money; we want to keep stress levels low so music remains a joy!
  • Christian Community: Our goal is to come together and share a witness of Jesus Christ, and our gratitude for Him, through uplifting music.

Our History:

Ascend Choir and Orchestra is a community ensemble founded in 2023 by producer Kerrie Weitzel. Its creation was motivated by a desire to provide a place for people in the Colorado Springs and Southern Colorado area to share their musical talents in a way that fits their busy lives. Ascend is an interfaith organization, and we owe a special thanks to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints for their ongoing support and use of their churches for rehearsals and performances.

The group began in the Fall of 2023 when Kerrie Weitzel conducted a choir to perform with the touring company of Michael McLean's Forgotten Carols. Driven by a desire to keep the music going, the organization expanded in the Spring of 2024 to include an orchestra for a performance of Rob Gardner's Lamb of God, also conducted by Weitzel. Lamb of God has since become an annual tradition, with subsequent performances in 2025 and 2026 under the baton of Scott Hart, who has served as the principal conductor ever since.

As the organization grew, a youth choir was added in August 2024. Directed by Alyssa Lincoln, the youth choir performs alongside the adult ensemble every fall and presents its own dedicated concert each spring. The leadership team expanded again when Alyssa Koogler was announced as the assistant choir conductor for the 2025 fall concert, a role she continues to hold today. Whether we are returning to perform the Forgotten Carols or putting on our annual concerts, Ascend continues to grow and bless the community with uplifting music.
